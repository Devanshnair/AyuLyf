Cumin(Jeera) water for Digestion:-
To make cumin water for digestion, boil a teaspoon of cumin seeds in a cup of water. Allow it to cool, strain, and drink. Cumin stimulates digestive enzymes, aiding in digestion and reducing bloating. It also has carminative properties, easing gas and promoting overall digestive health. Drink this infusion regularly for potential digestive benefits.

To make Amla (Indian Gooseberry) for immunity:
Extract the juice from fresh Amla fruits or consume it raw. Amla is rich in Vitamin C and antioxidants, which boost immune function by enhancing the production of white blood cells and neutralizing free radicals. It also helps in detoxification and supports overall health. Incorporating Amla into your diet regularly, whether through raw consumption, juice, or in the form of chyawanprash (an Ayurvedic herbal jam), may contribute to improved immunity.

To make Ajwain (Carom Seeds) for indigestion relief:
Crush 1 teaspoon of ajwain seeds and mix them with a pinch of black salt. Chew this mixture thoroughly after meals to aid digestion. Ajwain contains thymol, which helps in the secretion of gastric juices, thereby improving digestion and reducing bloating. It also has carminative properties that help in relieving gas and flatulence. This simple home remedy can be effective in promoting better digestion and providing relief from indigestion. If you have chronic digestive issues, it's advisable to consult a healthcare professional.

To make Turmeric Milk (Haldi Doodh), heat a cup of milk, add 1/2 to 1 teaspoon of turmeric powder, and sweeten to taste. Stir well and consume before bedtime.
Turmeric contains curcumin, known for its anti-inflammatory properties. When combined with warm milk, it creates a soothing drink believed to alleviate inflammation, promote healing, and boost the immune system. However, consult a healthcare professional for specific health concerns.
